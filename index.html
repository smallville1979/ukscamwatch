CV Tips That Actually Work

One page under 5 years experience. Two pages maximum. No photo. Bullet points not paragraphs. Tailor for every single job — copy words from the job advert directly into your CV.

⚡ Tailor every application
🎯

Cover Letters — Keep It Short

Three short paragraphs. Why you want this job. What you bring. When you're available. Shorter and specific beats longer and vague every single time.

✅ 3 paragraphs maximum
🤝

Interview Prep — STAR Method

Situation, Task, Action, Result. Prepare 5 examples from your life. Practice out loud — not in your head. Record yourself on your phone. Painful but it genuinely works.

🎤 Practice out loud always
💡 The one trick that works every time: Copy exact words from the job description into your CV. Applicant Tracking Systems scan for keyword matches before a human ever sees your application. If the job says "customer service experience" — use those exact words. Not "worked with customers." Exact words.
